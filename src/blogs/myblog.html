TOP PLACES ON MY BUCKET LIST THAT ARE WORTH EXPLORING!

 ~ 1st October 2022 by Keerthi

Why is it so difficult to choose a place to travel from millions of suggestions located all around the world?

   Sometimes we get overwhelmed by so many options that we see on the internet with no proper reason why that place is worth visiting. Most places that are being suggested out there are either very good at tourism spots but not so much for a getaway that one wishes to have on a vacation or they are great for a getaway but are underrated. Being a travel enthusiast myself who loves to make memories while exploring new places as well as go off into the wild and travel to places that are underrated but are worth exploring, here are some of the places at the top of my bucket list along with reasons why one should explore them at least once in their life.

# Places
1 The Swiss Alps, south of Zurich
2 Florence, Italy
3 palm Jumeriah, Dubai
4 The Holy City of Varanasi, India
5 Meteora in Greece
6 Great Pyramids at Giza

1.The Swiss Alps, south of Zurich


    If you want to have a budget-friendly hiking experience with an array of excellent trails that propels hikers from all corners of the globe then Switzerland’s alpine heart, the Swiss alps is the best choice for you. The Swiss alps, Switzerland's alpine heart, is your best option if you're looking for a hiking experience that won't break the bank and offers a variety of breathtaking trails that attracts hikers from around the world. Many picturesque small towns, like Grindelwald, can be found in the Swiss Alps, where visitors may get in touch with nature and learn a little bit about local Alpine culture. Additionally, you can experiment with the traditional Swiss Alps delicacy of melted cheese fondue, which is a mainstay of the local farmers' diet.



2.Florence, Italy


For all the history and art enthusiasts, this place will undoubtedly let you achieve your dream of being immersed in history, art, and architecture. One of Tuscany's most picturesque cities, Florence, is sometimes overlooked by tourists who are traveling the country. The most stunning masterpieces by historical artists such as Raphel, da vinci, Boteeicelli etc attracts millions of people from around the world If you base yourself in the city, it's simple to take quick day trips to the countryside to explore the area. Here, you can go wine tasting, explore medieval hilltop towns, rent a Vespa or bike and take in the scenery, enroll in a cooking class at a magnificent villa, or even support in the grape or olive extracting task.




3.Palm Jumeriah, Dubai

This place helps you fulfill your dreams of living a high life which will be an experience that you can remember for the rest of your life. This famous man-made island is a must-visit for people who wants to experience a wonderland filled with stunning architectural wonders and exciting experiences. These experiences include taking a speedboat tour around the island, exploring the Lost Chambers Aquarium, walking around the Al Ittihad park, taking up adventures with freefalling over the palm Jumeirah to witness the stunning views of palm Jumeirah, Dubai Marine, and the turquoise waters of the Arabian gulf and many more.




4.The Holy City of Varanasi, India


If you're searching for a place to experience spiritual awakening, positivity, or just a peaceful break, this is your one-stop destination. Due to its countless ghats, temples, captivating rituals, and vivid religious festivals that reflect Indian culture, Varanasi, a well-known tourist destination in India, draws visitors from all over the world. People travel great distances to attend Ganga aarti, which is known for being a transformative experience. The ambiance is filled with mesmerizing lighting diyas that are offered on the banks of the Ganges, sounds of bells, and smoke from incense and diyas.




5.Meteora in Greece


     Meteora is a place that is unlike anywhere else on earth. This can give you the experiences of a lifetime by hiking and exploring the distinctive rock formations that rise to the sky which are shelter the Greek religious orders for almost a thousand years. Visitors often overlook its typical historical values over its magnificent scenery. The geography, the history, and the culture of this area have their significance which can be understood by traveling along with a guide or local that can explain the significance of the landmarks such as the St George Madilas Cave. The mushroom museum is something you can’t miss while exploring Meteora. Delicious Greek food that is worth every penny and doesn’t worry mushrooms are the only item on their menu.




6.Great Pyramids at Giza


   The only surviving ancient wonder, along with ancient Egyptian statues and tombs that continue to seize the attention of archaeologists all around the world is The Great Pyramids of Giza. Even to this day, top archaeologists believe that only a little part of the ancient Egyptian civilization is being discovered and there is so much more left to be discovered and understood. Being one of the seven wonders of the ancient world is a perfect reminder of the ancient Egyptian glorification of life after death.
